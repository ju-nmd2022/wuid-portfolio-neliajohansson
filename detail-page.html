Ronja The Robber's Daughter Vector Design in Adobe Illustrator

One of my first projects in Adobe Illustrator was this simplistic poster of the classical Swedish children's book/movie Ronja The Robber's Daughter. My interpretation of the captivating fantasy saga, set in the early-Medieval Swedish woodlands, shows the silhouette of Ronja running around the thick forest with the harpies circulating the fort in the horizon.
